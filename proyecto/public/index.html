Beer and happiness

We decided to make our proyect around beer and happines.
We are curious about the data itself as we are sure as everyone else is. We think that happiness and alcohol consumption go hand in hand. With that in mind we are curious to see if this is true all across the world or if there are differences.

The plan is to:

  • Look at the Happiness Scores
  • Look at the Human Development Index (HDI)
  • Look at the GDP Per Capita
  • Look at the Alcohol consumption per Capita
